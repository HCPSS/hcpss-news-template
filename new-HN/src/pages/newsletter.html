Reminders

Virtual Graduations

Virtual commencement events for the HCPSS Class of 2020 take place through June 9. The events are streamed online at www.hcpss.org, and broadcast on HCPSS TV cable channels 42 (Verizon) and 95 (Comcast). If you aren't able to watch a virtual celebration in process, all graduation videos are archived for viewing on the Commencement webpage.

Free Meal Distribution

HCPSS will continue to provide free Grab-N-Go meals to anyone age 18 and under and/or any HCPSS student, regardless of age, throughout state-mandated school closures, extended through the end of the school year.

Last Day of School for Students

The Maryland State Department of Education has approved HCPSS' request for a waiver of five school days. As a result, the last day of school for students is Tuesday, June 23, 2020.

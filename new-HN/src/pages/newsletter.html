Internet Access

Comcast has opened free access to its Xfinity WiFi network, making hotspots across the country available to anyone, including non-subscribers, at no cost. Search by zip code or city to find a hotspot near you at wifi.xfinity.com. Comcast also provides low cost internet access to eligible families through its Comcast Internet Essentials program, including two months of free internet to new Internet Essential customers. The program is open to Free and Reduced Meals (FARMs) program participants, or those eligible for FARMs. Applications will be accepted through June 30.

Final Calendar Update

Tuesday, June 23

The Maryland State Department of Education has approved HCPSS’ request for a waiver of five school days. As a result, the last day of school for students is Tuesday, June 23, 2020. HCPSS will close out the school year with ample opportunities to focus on student social-emotional well-being and a structure for summer support.

Reminders

SMOB Election

The Howard County Association of Student Councils (HCASC) will hold its general election for the 2020-21 Student Member of the Board (SMOB) online June 2-3.

FY 2021 Budget

The Board has rescheduled adoption of the FY2021 Operating and Capital Budgets to June 11, 2020 at 3 p.m. A schedule of all upcoming Board meetings is available online.

Final Grades

Temporary grading modifications have been made to avoid penalizing students during emergency situations, such as the school closure caused by the coronavirus.

Free Meal Distribution

HCPSS will continue to provide free Grab-N-Go meals to anyone age 18 and under and/or any HCPSS student, regardless of age, throughout state-mandated school closures, extended through the end of the school year.

Summer Programs

In compliance with state and local guidelines, all HCPSS 2020 summer programs will be offered online. Virtual programs will have a modified schedule that will incorporate both live sessions and digital content that will be accessed online.

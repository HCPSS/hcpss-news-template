Med Sled Evacuation Devices Installation Complete

HCPSS recently installed cabinets to house a Med Sled emergency evacuation device to help transport individuals with mobility issues. The devices are now in place at every enclosed evacuation stairwell in all schools with multiple stories, with a total of 32 schools receiving 140 devices and cabinets.

Atholton Elementary Spreads Kindness Through Blankets

As an outgrowth of Atholton Elementary School’s recent kindness week, the 4th grade team wanted their students to experience showing kindness outside the school walls. All 85 Grade 4 students have since created 50 blankets for the local Project Linus nonprofit, whose mission is to provide homemade blankets “to children in hospitals, shelters, social service agencies or anywhere that a child might be in need of a big hug.”

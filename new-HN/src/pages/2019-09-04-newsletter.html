Events

Presentation: FY 2021 Capital Budget and FY 2022-2026 Capital Improvement Program

Tuesday, September 10, 5 p.m., Department of Education

Superintendent Martirano will present to the Board his proposed FY 2021 Capital Budget and FY 2022-2026 Capital Improvement Program. The presentation will be immediately followed by a Board of Education budget work session.

BSAP Saturday Math Academy Parent Information Session

Saturday, September 14, 9-10:30 a.m.,Oakland Mills High School

Parents/guardians of students in grades 3-12 are invited to learn more about the BSAP Saturday Math Academy. The information session will cover general information and the process for registration.

Boundary Review Feedback to the Board

September 17, 24 and 26, 7 p.m., Department of Education

The Board will gather feedback from the community during three boundary review public hearings, zoned by region. Pre-registration by phone or in person is required and begins three weeks before each event. Individuals may submit written testimony to redistricting@hcpss.org through November 19. Find full information online.

Public Hearing: FY 2021 Capital Budget and FY 2022-2026 Capital Improvement Program

Thursday, September 19, 7 p.m., Department of Education

The Board will hold a public hearing on the Superintendent's proposed FY 2021 Capital Budget and FY 2022-2026 Capital Improvement Program. Learn how you can provide testimony.

Interested in Becoming a Bus Driver?

Independent school bus contractors provide bus transportation for HCPSS students and have immediate openings.

Check Your Canvas Notification Status

Parents can customize the volume, the medium (email and text) and the frequency of communications regarding their student’s courses.

Update Emergency Contact Information with Family File

Each year parents/guardians need to verify or update information for each child, including student information, contacts, medical, arrival and dismissals, photo use preferences and more. Access Family File by logging in to HCPSS Connect, selecting the Family File tab on the left and then selecting Family File. Details and resources can be found on the HCPSS website.

Emergency and Inclement Weather Notifications

In times of emergency/weather closings, information is shared through our website, social media, text, email, weather hotline and cable TV. Complete information and a full list of HCPSS emergency notification sources can be found online.

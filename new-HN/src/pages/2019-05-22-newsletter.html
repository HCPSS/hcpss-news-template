Pointers Run Celebrates a Day of Art

Pointers Run Elementary School students recently enjoyed Art Extravaganza Day, a day immersed in visual and performing arts. More than 100 National Art Honor Society high school students led original art workshops, and Clarksville Middle and Pointers Run orchestras performed.

2019 Summer Food Service Program

Beginning Tuesday, June 25 and continuing through August, HCPSS will serve free meals at 15 locations to help fill the nutritional gap for hungry children and families over the summer months. Learn more.

2019 High School Graduations

Commencement ceremonies will be held May 23-30. All ceremonies will be streamed live on the HCPSS website, and available on-demand following each graduation. All graduation-related information, including parking details and maps, how to view, and video/photo guidelines can be found online.

Parent Canvas Satisfaction Survey

Families are invited to take a brief survey about their experience using the Canvas Learning Management System. The anonymous survey is open until 11:59 p.m. on Monday, June 3. Students also are invited to take a survey geared toward their experiences.

Connect Outage May 23

HCPSS Connect will be unavailable Thursday, May 23 from 6-8 p.m. while updates are made. During this time parents will not be able to access Connect, including Canvas and Synergy, however students will be able to access Canvas. Once the update is complete, users may continue to log in as usual.

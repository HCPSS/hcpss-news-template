HCPSS Receives A+ on School Food Environment Grades Report

HCPSS was the only Maryland school system to earn an A+ and only one of two to earn an A on the 2018-19 School Food Environment Grades report from Healthy School Food Maryland. Howard County ranked highest overall in the state for the third consecutive year.

Summer Meal Site Update

Meals previously served at the Wilde Lake Tennis Club are now provided at the Dasher Green Pool on weekdays, from 12:30-1:30 p.m. Menus for all 15 county locations are available online. View the complete list of summer meal locations.

Boundary Review Online Resources

The HCPSS Boundary Review process began in June; regular updates are provided online. Visit the site to find Attendance Area Committee (AAC) meeting presentations and minutes, community input opportunities, information about the process and answers to frequently asked questions. The Boundary Review process overview document is now available online in Spanish, Korean and Chinese.

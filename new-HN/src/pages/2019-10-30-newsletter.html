Impact Aid Forms

Parents/guardians who work on a federal property in Maryland and/or are a member of a uniformed service can qualify HCPSS to receive additional federal funding. Parents who completed Family File and who identified themselves as working on federal property in Maryland or as active duty military will receive pre-filled Impact Aid forms by November 8, to confirm the information and provide a signature. Signed forms returned to your child’s school will help ensure that the school system receives applicable funding.

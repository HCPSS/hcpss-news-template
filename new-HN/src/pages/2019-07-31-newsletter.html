Boundary Review Public Hearings

September 17, 24 and 26, 7 p.m., Department of Education

The Board of Education will hold three public hearings to gather feedback from the community. Members of the public may attend only the session associated with the region in which they live based on the School Locator. Pre-registration opens three weeks prior to each hearing date and is available by phone or in person. There will be no online pre-registration or on-site registration at a hearing. Alternatively, individuals may submit testimony via email or mail. Find full details online.

Staff Focus: Donald Felch, CMSgt, USAF (Ret), JROTC Aerospace Science Instructor

“Three years ago, I found Oakland Mills Air Force JROTC [Junior Reserve Officer Training Corps] and discovered I could combine my love for developing young leaders with my military background and civilian education,” Chief Master Sergeant Donald Felch said. JROTC is an elective class open to HCPSS high school students throughout the county, with no commitment to join the military. The program teaches citizenship, leadership and life skills that translate into success after high school. Learn more about JROTC and its application process at tomorrow’s information session.

Boundary Review Update

The Board of Education continues to hold Public Work Sessions to work towards a final plan on boundary adjustments that could impact communities in any of the 74 HCPSS schools for the 2020–2021 school year. Upcoming Board Public Work Sessions are scheduled on November 12, 4 p.m.; November 13, 1 p.m.; November 14, 1 and 6:30 p.m.; and November 18, 6:30 p.m. The Board is scheduled to make a final decision on boundary adjustments for the 2020-2021 school year on November 21 at 7 p.m.

Weekly Updates

11/9/2018

Information and Resources:

  • Thank you school administrators for your time and participation yesterday on the FARMs program and ESSA communications planning. Please be sure to check out the Edutopia article and FARMs handout that was shared yesterday and please don’t hesitate to connect with Jahantab Siddiqui or Brian Bassett with any additional thoughts.
  • Remember: Families may apply for FARMs at any time of the year. We have an October 31 deadline for reporting to the state but students and families can begin accessing the benefits at any time of the year once they apply and are confirmed.
  • The Free and Reduced-Priced Meals (FARMs) program is not just about free and reduced food for qualifying families- it provides access to an array of services for qualified families including free SAT and ACT registration, free tuition to take college courses at Howard Community College while still enrolled in high school, free college applications, reduced AP exam fees, reduced costs for a number of services provided by Howard County Rec and Parks, Columbia Association and Comcast, and much more. Visit the HCPSS website for more information and to apply.
  • Please be sure to notify your families when parent-teacher conference registration will open (or if it is already open) and how to register in order to prevent misunderstanding or misinformation in the community.
  • FYI Tweeters! Use #CelebrateHCPSS when tweeting celebrations in your school or office. We also want to celebrate your celebrations!

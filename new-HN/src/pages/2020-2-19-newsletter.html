Grant Program Opens Equitable Career Path for Paraeducators

The Para to Teacher Pathway program, offered in partnership with Bowie State University and the Howard County Education Association (HCEA), will enable HCPSS paraeducators to earn teacher certification while continuing in their current roles and receiving professional learning and other support.

Update on Summer Program Registration

The February window for summer program registration has closed. Requests are now being reviewed, and all students will be placed in the course of their selection or on a waitlist, and notified by email by February 29. The March window for on-site and online enrollment requests begins Monday, March 2 at 6 p.m.

A Story Called Kindness Poster Contest

Entries Due April 1

All students in grades K–12 are invited to design a poster to illustrate a scene from your life story where you’ve shown someone kindness or offered a helping hand.

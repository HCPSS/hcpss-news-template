Staff Focus: Supporting English Language Learners While Embracing Self-Care

At Oakland Mills Middle School, Sarah Mayhew supports English language learners by keeping in touch with families, co-teaching classes, and collaborating with teachers to provide differentiated learning opportunities and incorporate diverse texts into the curriculum. Mayhew is also her school’s well-being representative for staff. Mayhew explained, “When we’re good to ourselves, that transcends into the classroom and school culture.”

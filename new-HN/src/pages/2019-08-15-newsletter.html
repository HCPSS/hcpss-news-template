August 15, 2019

Get Ready for the 2019-20 School Year!

We’re closing in on the end of summer. Visit the HCPSS website for what you need to know for the 2019-2020 school year, including:

School Calendar

Print-friendly and online calendars are available on our website, and will reflect the most up-to-date information. Users also may subscribe to the HCPSS calendar and add it to the service of your choosing. There will be no printed 2019-2020 calendars.

View calendars

Immunizations

All children must have the required State of Maryland immunizations in order to attend school. Students out of compliance or without evidence of an appointment for necessary immunizations may not be admitted to school.


Find details

Emergency Card Procedures with Family File

HCPSS utilizes Family File for parents/guardians to complete emergency information and share preferences. Information must be updated for each child. Log into to HCPSS Connect, select the Family File tab and then click on Family File. Detailed instructions are online.

Log in to HCPSS Connect

Bus Schedules

Our bus stop locator is now updated. Users can locate bus stops by entering their street address and zip code in the search field.

Use the locator

School Supplies

Each elementary and middle school provides its own school supply list, compiled on the HCPSS website for your convenience. High school students will be given lists by their teachers at the start of the year. Please contact your child’s school with any questions.

View supply lists

Back to School Nights

The back to school night schedule for all HCPSS schools is available online. Last-minute changes may not be reflected online, so please double check dates and times with your child’s school.

See the schedule

Student Drivers

All student drivers and a parent or guardian must attend a safe driving program in order for the student to obtain a parking permit. Students may attend a presentation at any location.

Attend a session

School Meals

Our Food and Nutrition Services webpage has information about meal prices, how to pay for meals, where to view menus, nutritional and allergen information, and much more.

Learn more

Free and Reduced Meals Program

Participation in the FARMs program also qualifies students for additional opportunities and services. Parents must submit a new application every school year.

Learn more and apply

Emergency Notifications

In times of emergency/weather closings, information is shared through our website, social media, text, email, weather hotline and HCPSS cable TV. Complete information and a full list of HCPSS emergency notification sources can be found online.

Find details

" }} {{> news-item title="March to the Future: Dispelling the Myths Associated with HBCUs" message="

Wednesday, Feb. 21, 7 p.m., virtual

Led by Hammond High School Career Readiness Advisor Monica Stevens, this online session will feature a discussion with a professor from Virginia State University on the benefits of attending an HBCU. It also will include a panel of students who will dispel myths typically associated with HBCUs.

" }} {{> news-item title="March to the Future: Military Information Night" message="

Wednesday, March 6, 7 p.m., virtual

Representatives from each branch of the United States military will present information about their mission and specialty. Participants will learn about educational and career pathways, the GI bill, ROTC opportunities, and more. Additionally, representatives from military academies will discuss their unique application process. The session will include a student panel representing all of the military pathways who will share their experiences and lessons learned.

Registration is required. Additional March to the Future events can be found online.

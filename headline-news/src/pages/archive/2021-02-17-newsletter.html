 {{> news-item title="Changes to 2020-2021 Academic Calendar" message="

Thursday, February 25, and Friday, February 26 will be early dismissal days for students at all levels. The early dismissals will provide school-based staff with additional time to complete classroom setup and other preparations necessary to ensure a smooth transition to a hybrid model of in-person and concurrent learning.

" }} {{> news-item title="Budget Basics Virtual Workshops" message="

Students in grades 3-12 and their families are invited to learn about electronic and virtual financial transactions and key financial principles in this three-part series of virtual workshops, co-sponsored by HCPSS and The Council of Elders. The first workshop is being held February 20 from 9-10:30 a.m. Register online to receive a confirmation email with information about joining the workshops.

" }} {{> news-item title="Hiring School Bus Drivers, Substitute and Temporary Employees" message="

HCPSS is hiring substitute teachers, classroom monitors, and lunch and recess monitors in preparation for students and staff returning to hybrid in-person instruction beginning March 1, 2021. Independent school bus contractors provide bus transportation for HCPSS students, and are hiring for immediate openings to prepare for students returning to schools. To ensure school bus safety in the COVID-19 era, all buses will be disinfected after each route, and all bus drivers and attendants are being trained in safe procedures.

" }} {{> news-item title="All School Start Times and Student Schedules to Change March 1" message="

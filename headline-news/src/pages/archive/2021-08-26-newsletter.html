 {{> news-item title="Bus Schedules Available Online" message="

The 2021-2022 bus stop locator is available online. Users can locate bus stops by entering the student's grade, street address and zip code in the search menu. Updates to the site are still being made, and parents are advised to double check the site this weekend to verify bus stops, numbers and times. Families may notice earlier pickup or later drop-off times than expected, which reflects the need to extend some routes due to severe driver shortages.

 {{> news-item title="Apply for FARMs Now to Earn P-EBT Benefits" message="

Participation in FARMs qualifies students for a wide range of opportunities and resources beyond school meals. Qualified families are encouraged to apply by August 27 to earn federal P-EBT benefits for July and August 2021. Families who do not currently qualify for FARMs now, but whose circumstances may change in the future can reapply for FARMs at any time during the school year.

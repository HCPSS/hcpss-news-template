 {{> news-item title="Friends of Education Award" message="

The Howard County Board of Education recognized Ying Matties with its prestigious Friends of Education Award during its April 7 meeting. Matties founded the Community Allies of Rainbow Youth (CARY) organization and works tirelessly to help improve policies, practices and curriculum and make them more inclusive, accepting and relevant.

" }} {{> news-item title="Modifications to 2021-2022 Calendar and 2022 Summer Programs" message="

The original HCPSS calendar had schools and offices closed June 28 for primary elections. However the Maryland Court of Appeals moved the primary elections to July 19 and, in response, the Board of Education approved the following calendar modification:

  • Tuesday, June 28 – Schools and offices open (previously closed)
  • Tuesday, July 19 – Schools and offices closed

Due to the calendar modification and other scheduling requirements, several changes were made to the 2022 Summer Programs, including updated start/end times for the BSAP Summer Institute, Innovative Pathways High School and STARTALK Chinese Language Summer Camp.

" }} {{> news-item title="Pre-K Registration is Now Open" message="

Registration for 2022-23 prekindergarten is now open. The HCPSS pre-K curriculum, taught by certified staff, supports early learning and school readiness and provides experiences that foster children’s academic, social, emotional and physical development. Children must be four-years-old by Sept. 1, 2022, to be considered for enrollment. Additional information about the pre-K program, eligibility, and the application process is available online.

" }} {{> news-item title="HCPSS and HCEA Ratify Master Agreement" message="

HCPSS and the Howard County Education Association have ratified their master agreement for the 2022-2023 school year. The new agreement provides step and cost-of-living increases to employees and raises the starting salary for teachers, among other changes.

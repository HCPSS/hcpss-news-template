 {{> news-item title="Celebrating HCPSS National Board Certified Teachers" message="

Seventy-six HCPSS teachers achieved National Board certification in December 2022—22 first-time achievers and 54 renewers. National Board Certification is the highest-level credential in the educational profession. Howard County is among the top five Maryland districts by historical number of NBCTs.

" }} {{> news-item title="Important Health & Safety Message" message="

All HCPSS students and staff must stay home if they are experiencing symptom(s) associated with any communicable disease.

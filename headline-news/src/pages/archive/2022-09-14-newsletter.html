 {{> news-item title="Superintendent’s Proposed Capital Budget" message="

HCPSS Superintendent Martirano last week presented his Proposed Capital Budget for fiscal year 2024 (FY 2024), FY 2025–2029 Capital Improvement Program, and FY 2024–2033 Long-Range Master Plan to the Howard County Board of Education. The proposal reflects projected student capacity and existing facility needs in order to relieve overcrowded schools and support rapid enrollment growth.

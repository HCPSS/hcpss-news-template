 {{> news-item title="Update on Installation of HEPA Filters and Outdoor Tents" message="

HCPSS is acquiring and installing HEPA air filter units in all classrooms at all levels, as well as tents to increase outdoor dining spaces at all elementary schools. Vendor deliveries of all remaining HEPA filters are expected by October 1, and vendor delivery of the tents is expected by September 24. Both the filters and the tents are being installed in order of school capacity utilization.

" }} {{> news-item title="COVID Staff Testing Program Update" message="

Due to the complexity of preparations for the staff testing program vendor, the COVID staff testing program is now scheduled to launch during the week of October 4. In addition, visitors and volunteers who enter HCPSS schools to interact with or support students will need to provide proof of vaccination status or a negative weekly COVID test beginning October 4.

" }} {{> news-item title="COVID-19 Self-Screenings" message="

All parents/guardians are to perform a COVID-19 health check every day before your student leaves home. Students are not to come to school if they are experiencing any symptom(s) associated with COVID-19 or other communicable disease.

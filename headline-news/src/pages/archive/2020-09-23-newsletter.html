 {{> news-item title="Free Student Meals" message="

The food services program during the first semester of the 2020-2021 school year consists of Grab-and-Go meals served every day school is in session, Monday through Friday from 8-9 a.m. and 10:45 a.m.-12:45 p.m. There will be no meals when school is closed on Monday, September 28. Meals are free for all students through December.

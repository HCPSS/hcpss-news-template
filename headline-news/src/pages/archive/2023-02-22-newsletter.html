 {{> news-item title="Board Adopts Requested FY 2024 Operating and Capital Budgets" message="

The Board of Education unanimously adopted its Operating and Capital Budget Requests for the 2023-2024 school year during its meeting on Feb. 16. The request keeps the Superintendent’s proposed FY 2024 operating budget intact while adding a number of priorities identified by the Board. The Board’s budget will be submitted to the County Executive the week of Feb. 27.

 {{> news-item title="Unheard Perspectives Showcase" message="

This annual event, being held on Saturday, Feb. 25 from 9 a.m.-1 p.m. at Hammond Middle School, is the culmination of weeks of research by third, fourth, and fifth grade students about innovators whose voices have been underrepresented in American history. This year’s showcase will feature exhibits from the top three student teams from 17 HCPSS elementary schools.

" }} {{> news-item title="Important Health & Safety Message" message="

All HCPSS students and staff must stay home if they are experiencing symptom(s) associated with any communicable disease. Families, students and staff should perform daily health checks and stay home if they have symptoms of a communicable disease, including the seasonal flu, COVID-19 and Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV). Families are encouraged to consider taking advantage of flu and COVID vaccines and boosters.

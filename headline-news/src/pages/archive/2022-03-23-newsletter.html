 {{> news-item title="Students Advance to 2022 State History Day Competition" message="

Earlier this month, HCPSS students in grades 6-12 competed in HCPSS’ annual History Day event, conducting research and presenting on this year’s National History Day theme, “Debate and Diplomacy in History.” The top two in each of five categories will compete in the statewide contest in April. Additionally, Lime Kiln Middle School Gifted and Talented resource teacher Tracy Spillman was named the Howard County History Day Teacher of the Year.

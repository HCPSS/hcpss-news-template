 {{> news-item title="Hiring School Bus Drivers, Substitute and Temporary Employees" message="

HCPSS is hiring substitute teachers, classroom monitors, and lunch and recess monitors in preparation for students and staff returning to hybrid in-person instruction beginning March 1, 2021. Independent school bus contractors provide bus transportation for HCPSS students, and are hiring for immediate openings to prepare for students returning to schools. To ensure school bus safety in the COVID-19 era, all buses will be disinfected after each morning, noon, and afternoon routes, and all bus drivers and attendants are being trained in safe procedures.

 {{> news-item title="Deadline Extended: Parent Commitment for Remainder of 2020-2021 School Year Due Feb. 10" message="

Parents/guardians must make a commitment regarding sending their child to school for all or part of the week (depending on program availability) and receive virtual instruction the remainder of the week, or to have their child remain fully virtual for the remainder of the 2020-2021 school year.

  • Monday, Dec. 19 at 9 p.m. The window will last until all necessary updates have been successfully applied but not beyond Tuesday, Dec, 10 at 5 a.m. During this time, parents/guardians may not be able to access or experience a sluggish response when trying to access Connect, including Canvas and Synergy. " }} {{> news-item title="Asynchronous Instruction Plan Inclement Weather Days" message="

With the ongoing potential of wintry weather, families are encouraged to review the HCPSS Inclement Weather Asynchronous Instruction Plan. Families will be notified about the asynchronous instruction day the same as all other inclement weather notifications. Asynchronous instruction day materials for elementary school students are posted on the HCPSS website. Middle and high school students have been provided an HCPSS-issued device and will access assignments via Canvas. All students will have up to 10 days following the asynchronous day of instruction to complete this work.

" }} {{> news-item title="Important Health & Safety Message" message="

All HCPSS students and staff must stay home if they are experiencing symptom(s) associated with any communicable disease. Families, students and staff should perform daily health checks and stay home if they have symptoms of a communicable disease, including the seasonal flu, COVID-19 and Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV). Families are encouraged to consider taking advantage of flu and COVID vaccines and boosters.

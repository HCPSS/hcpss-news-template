 {{> news-item title="Redistricting Process to Open New High School #13 Finalized" message="

The Board of Education last week adopted new boundaries for the 2023-2024 school year to accommodate the opening of New High School #13. The online school locator tool has been updated to reflect the new attendance areas for next school year. FAQs about the 2023-23 redistricting process also have been updated.

" }} {{> news-item title="Rescheduled: Public Hearing: Naming of New High School #13" message="

The Board of Education public hearing on the naming of New High School #13, originally scheduled for Dec. 5, has been rescheduled for Thursday, Dec. 15 at 7 p.m. Community members can sign up through Dec. 14 and must indicate if they wish to speak in person or virtually when completing the registration.

" }} {{> news-item title="Class of 2023 Graduations, May 18-June 1, 2023" message="

HCPSS has confirmed the window for the 2023 high school graduations as Thursday, May 18 – Thursday, June 1, 2023. Ceremonies will be held in person at Merriweather Post Pavilion, with the exception of Cedar Lane School, which will be held at the school.

" }} {{> news-item title="Important Health & Safety Message" message="

All HCPSS students and staff must stay home if they are experiencing symptom(s) associated with any communicable disease. Families, students and staff should perform daily health checks and stay home if they have symptoms of a communicable disease, including the seasonal flu, COVID-19 and Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV). Families are encouraged to consider taking advantage of flu and COVID vaccines and boosters.

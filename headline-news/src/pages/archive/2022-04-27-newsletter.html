" }} {{> news-item title="Parent/Guardian Canvas Dashboard Update" message="

This afternoon, Canvas will update its parent/guardian dashboard view so that it displays the same dashboard interface students see. This change will only impact parents and guardians of high school students who also have a student in elementary or middle school when viewing Canvas via a web browser. Selecting an elementary or middle school child will show the K-8 dashboard layout, and selecting a high school child will show the Classic Canvas dashboard. In the Classic Canvas layout, student grades can be viewed by selecting the Grades icon in the left navigation bar or the View Grades on the lower left of the dashboard. The Canvas Parent Mobile App provides at-a-glance access to grades and assignments, and is available from the Android and iOS app stores.

 {{> news-item title="Fall Planning Update" message="

The Board of Education approved a fully virtual first semester for the 2020-2021 school year, which runs from September 8, 2020 through January 28, 2021. Students at all levels will engage in four days of teacher-led synchronous (live) instruction each week, and complete asynchronous assignments on the fifth day. Updated information is provided on the HCPSS website.

Under the revised calendar, Tuesday, September 8 will be the first student day, and school staff will begin on August 25.

" }} {{> news-item title="Maryland Feminine Hygiene Products Law" message="

The State of Maryland recently established a law requiring public schools to provide menstrual hygiene products via dispensers in school restrooms at no cost to students. Accordingly, and per requirements in Maryland HB 205 and SB 427, HCPSS has complied with the requirement that dispensers were to be installed in at least two women’s restrooms per school.

" }} {{> news-item title="Free and Reduced Meals Program" message="

Families are encouraged to apply for the federal Free and Reduced Meals (FARMs) program, which encompasses more than free meals. Participation may also qualify students for free/reduced registration for SAT/ACT/AP, reduced program fees, discounted internet and computers, free college applications and more. Qualification is based on family size and income, and enrollment is confidential. Families can apply for free and reduced meals online. A new application is required every year.

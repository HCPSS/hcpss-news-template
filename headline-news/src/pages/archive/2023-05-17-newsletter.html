" }} {{> news-item title="High School Graduations Begin May 24" message="

" }} {{> news-item title="Families Must Register for Bus Service by June 1" message="

Families whose children are eligible for bus transportation must register through HCPSS Connect Synergy to receive bus service for the 2023-2024 school year. Instructions on how to register students are available online. Families who reside in non-transportation areas and families of students who receive transportation as part of their Individualized Education Program (IEP) or 504 Plan do not need to take any action.

" }} {{> news-item title="Provide Feedback on Inclement Weather Asynchronous Instruction Plan" message="

For the 2023-2024 school year, HCPSS again will repurpose up to three inclement weather days as asynchronous instruction days, which will be used at the discretion of the Superintendent. All parents, students, staff and community members are invited to provide feedback on the plan by emailing InclementWeatherPlan@hcpss.org through June 9, 2023.

 {{> news-item title="HCPSS Seeks Members for Policy Advisory Committees" message="

HCPSS is currently seeking candidates for membership on several School Year 2023-2024 Policy Advisory Committees. " }} {{> news-item title="Free Summer Meal Program Begins June 21" message="

HCPSS will run a free summer meal program weekdays June 21-Aug. 4. Meals will be served only on days schools are open; there will be no meal service Tuesday, July 4. There are no applications, enrollment or fees to participate in this program, but children must be present to receive free meals. Additional information, including a list of meal sites and pickup times, is available online.

" }} {{> news-item title="Important Health & Safety Message" message="

All HCPSS students and staff must stay home if they are experiencing symptom(s) associated with any communicable disease. Families, students and staff should perform daily health checks and stay home if they have symptoms of a communicable disease, including the seasonal flu, COVID-19 and Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV). Families are encouraged to consider taking advantage of flu and COVID vaccines and boosters.

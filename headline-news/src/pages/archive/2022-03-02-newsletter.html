" }} {{> news-item title="Record High Graduation Rate for Class of 2021" message="

" }} {{> news-item title="2022-2023 Kindergarten Registration Kickoff" message="

Kindergarten registration for families of children who will be 5 by September 1, 2022, kicks off Monday, March 7. Parents can begin the enrollment process online, after which your child’s school will contact you to complete the registration process. Current HCPSS prekindergarten students do not need to be re-registered for kindergarten because they are already enrolled as an HCPSS student.

" }} {{> news-item title="Board Adopts Requested FY 2023 Operating and Capital Budgets" message="

At its Feb. 24 meeting, the Board of Education adopted its requested FY 2023 Operating & Capital Budgets, which will be submitted to the Howard County Executive for his consideration. The Board’s request, totaling $1.1 billion, reflects the school system’s funding needs to begin implementing Maryland’s Blueprint for the Future and continue supporting students in the face of the challenges created by the pandemic.

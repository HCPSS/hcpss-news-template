 {{> news-item title="Current In-Person Opportunities Suspended; In-Person Athletic Conditioning Canceled" message="

Due to the rapid escalation of COVID-19 cases in Howard County, and in consultation with the Howard County Health Department, HCPSS has suspended all current in-person opportunities, with participating students returning to a fully virtual environment beginning Thursday, November 19. Additionally, in-person athletic conditioning, previously scheduled to begin on November 16, has been canceled.

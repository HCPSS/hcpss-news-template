" }} {{> news-item title="Apprenticeship Maryland Academy Info Session" message="

Tuesday, March 7, 6:00 p.m., Applications and Research Laboratory

Current 11th grade students and their parents/guardians are invited to attend an in-person information session on the HCPSS Apprenticeship Maryland Academy. Participants will learn how the Academy provides high school students opportunities to enter the workforce while still in high school, gain marketable industry skills and receive hands-on career training through a paid, on-the-job training program.

" }} {{> news-item title="Annual SMOB Convention" message="

Tuesday, March 21, 9 a.m.-1 p.m., Wilde Lake High School

The Howard County Association of Student Councils (HCASC) will host its annual Student Member of the Board of Education (SMOB) Convention on Tuesday, March 21. Organized and led by HCASC members, the event will bring together 138 delegates representing all HCPSS middle and high schools for a series of activities designed to familiarize them with the candidates competing to become the 2023-2024 SMOB.

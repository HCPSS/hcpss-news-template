HCPSS has developed an extensive School Readiness Checklist to prepare school facilities for the transition to hybrid in-person and concurrent virtual learning. The checklist, which includes 25 individual inspection items, is being used during walk-through inspections to ensure that all preparations are complete before students begin to return on Monday, March 1. Results of the completed inspections are posted on the Facilities Checklist site.

 {{> news-item title="New Student Schedules Go into Effect Monday, March 1" message="

All student schedules will change effective Monday, March 1, regardless of whether the student is returning in-person or remaining virtual. School start times have been pushed back 20 minutes. General student schedules are available online. Schools will communicate group placements in the coming days. Additional information and resources to aid families and students in the transition to hybrid and concurrent virtual learning are available online. HCPSS’ Help site also provides answers to hundreds of questions about hybrid learning, health and safety, transportation, and more.

 {{> news-item title="Update to Student Meal Service" message="

Effective March 1, meals will be available for pickup from 7:45-8:45 a.m. at elementary school locations and 9-10 a.m. at middle and high school locations on specified days. There will be no pickups on Tuesdays and Fridays. Students and parents/guardians will continue to have the option to receive meals at their child’s school or at any designated meal distribution site. Additional details about changes to meal service are available online.

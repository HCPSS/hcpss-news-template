 {{> news-item title="HCPSS Schools Honored as 2023 Maryland Green Schools" message="

Patapsco Middle School and Worthington Elementary School, became newly certified as Maryland Green Schools, by the Maryland Association for Environmental and Outdoor Education (MAEOE). Additionally, Homewood Center was recognized as recertified and Mount View Middle School and Veterans Elementary School achieved Sustainable School Status, having participated in the program for 14 years. HCPSS has a total of 42 schools participating in the Maryland Green Schools program.

" }} {{> news-item title="Columbia Community Care Partnership Builds Life Skills" message="

Through a new partnership between Columbia Community Care (CCC) and the Homewood Center, CCC will provide a group mentorship program for students focused on life skills. CCC also will support Homewood’s Beyond the 21 program by recruiting speakers for the Career Speaker Series and local businesses to host student field trips.

" }} {{> news-item title="Howard County Youth Orchestra/Guster Tickets On Sale Now" message="

Tickets are on sale now for the Howard County Youth Orchestra’s (HCYO) performance with alternative rock-band Guster, being held at Merriweather Post Pavilion on Sunday, May 21 at 7:30 p.m. HCYO is HCPSS’ premier high school performing ensemble.

" }} {{> news-item title="Register by June 1 for 2023-2024 Bus Service" message="

Families whose students are eligible for HCPSS-provided transportation are required to register by June 1 to receive bus service for the 2023-2024 school year. Families who reside in non-transportation areas and those who have students who receive transportation as part of their IEP or 504 Plan do not need to take any action.

" }} {{> news-item title="Important Health & Safety Message" message="

All HCPSS students and staff must stay home if they are experiencing symptom(s) associated with any communicable disease. Families, students and staff should perform daily health checks and stay home if they have symptoms of a communicable disease, including the seasonal flu, COVID-19 and Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV). Families are encouraged to consider taking advantage of flu and COVID vaccines and boosters.

" }} {{> news-item title="HCPSS Seeks Members for LGBTQIA+ Advisory Committee, Apply Oct. 21" message="

HCPSS seeks members to participate on its LGBTQIA+ Advisory Committee. Community members of all sexual orientations and gender identities who have an interest in supporting LGBTQIA+ HCPSS students are encouraged to apply for participation by Oct. 21.

" }} {{> news-item title="FY 2022 Annual Comprehensive Financial Report, Annual Audit Completion and Elimination of the Historic Health and Dental Fund Deficit" message="

HCPSS received an unmodified opinion from its external auditor, CohnReznick LLP, signifying that the HCPSS financial statements are presented fairly and in accordance with generally accepted accounting principles. More significantly, the opinion reflects HCPSS’ focus on improving the financial management of the school system through the elimination of its Health and Dental Fund deficit.

When schools have an unscheduled/emergency early dismissal, students are sent home using the departure method provided by parents/guardians in Family File. Parents/guardians should review the information selected for their child and make changes as necessary. (In Family File, select Arrival/Departure from the navigation pane on the left, and then expand the section for each student listed to view the departure method indicated under the heading “If there is an unscheduled early closing of school.”)

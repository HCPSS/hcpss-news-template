 {{> news-item title="2021-2022 High School Course Registration Open Through Jan. 15" message="

Current 8-11th grade students and their parents/guardians may view their teachers’ course recommendations and submit course placement requests for the 2021-2022 school year through HCPSS Connect. Course requests and alternates should be submitted by Friday, January 15, 2021. Additional information and resources to assist in course selection are available online.

" }} {{> news-item title="Technology Support Centers" message="

HCPSS is currently operating two Technology Support Centers for device exchanges, returns and support: Old Bushy Park Elementary School, Room E2 and Applications Research Laboratory (ARL), Room B37. Hours of operation are Monday, Wednesday and Friday from 7:30 a.m.-12:30 p.m., and Tuesday and Thursday from 2-5 p.m. The Technology Support Centers are closed any day that HCPSS offices are closed. The HCPSS Logistics Center and Ascend One facility are no longer available for technology support.

" }} {{> news-item title="Winter Athletics Season Canceled" message="

Due to continuing high rates of COVID-19 infection in Howard County, the 2020-21 winter athletics season has been canceled. Winter athletic coaches will continue to engage and support student-athletes virtually through the scheduled conclusion of the season on February 13.

" }} {{> news-item title="Free Student Meals" message="

Grab-and-Go meals are served every day school is in session, Monday through Friday from 8-9 a.m. and 10:45 a.m.-12:45 p.m., and are free of charge for all students and any child under age 18. All meals include breakfast, lunch, snacks and dinner. Find details about pre-ordering, weekend meals and more information online.

Students at Lime Kiln Middle School and Mt. Hebron High School made it to the state finals in this year’s Samsung “Solve for Tomorrow” contest. LKMS developed a plan to create a sensor to monitor waste collected at the end of a drain pipe, while MHHS’s plan featured the creation of a device that would enable drivers to share their license and registration information with police officers electronically, over a wireless connection.

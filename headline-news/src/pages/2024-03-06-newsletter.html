" }} {{> news-item title="HCPSS Students Take Top Spots in State Culinary Competition" message="

Four teams from HCPSS’ Culinary Science Academy recently participated in the Maryland ProStart Student Invitational (MPSI), a statewide team culinary and hospitality management competition. Teams from Howard and Wilde Lake high schools placed second and third, respectively, in the Management portion of the competition. A second Wilde Lake team and a team from River Hill High School participated in the Culinary portion of the competition, with River Hill’s team placing third.

" }} {{> news-item title="HCPSS Students Mark Global Unplugging Day" message="

Six HCPSS students/members of Howard County’s Youth Climate Institute joined Maryland first lady Dawn Moore, Maryland Department of Natural Resources Secretary Josh Kurtz, Maryland Department of Health Secretary Dr. Laura Herrera Scott, and mental health and environmental experts at Patapsco Valley State Park on March 1 to mark the Global Day of Unplugging. The annual event encourages people around the world to take a 24-hour break from digital activity to improve their health and human connections.

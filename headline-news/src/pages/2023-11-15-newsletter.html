--- subject: HCPSS News --- {{> header date="November 15, 2023"}} {{> hero title="HCPSS Celebrates Largest Multilingual Parent Leadership Program Cohort" imageAlt="A group photo of the graduates of the HCPSS Multilingual Parent Leadership Program." image="https://news.hcpss.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/mplp2023-500.jpg" message="

Thirty-four HCPSS parents/guardians recently graduated from HCPSS’ Multilingual Parent Leadership Program, aimed at helping multilingual parents navigate the school system, develop the confidence to serve as advocates for their children, and prepare for leadership roles in schools and the community. The cohort is the largest in the program’s 17-year history. Congratulations to these amazing members of the HCPSS community!

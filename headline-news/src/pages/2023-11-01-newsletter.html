Parents/guardians who work on a federal property in Maryland and/or are a member of a uniformed service can qualify HCPSS to receive federal funding by completing the Impact Aid form online by Dec. 14. " }} {{> news-item title="Math Teachers Take Home Top Honors" message="

Scott Boettinger of Elkridge Landing Middle School and Nina Diehl of Murray Hill Middle School were recently named the 2023 Outstanding Beginning Mathematics Teacher and Outstanding Middle School Mathematics Teacher, respectively, by the Maryland Council of Teachers of Mathematics. They were recognized based on their excellence in mathematics education and their implementation of effective and research-informed practices in their daily work with students, staff, and families.

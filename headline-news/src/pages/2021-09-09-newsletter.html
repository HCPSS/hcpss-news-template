 {{> news-item title="Vaccination Expectations for Volunteers" message="

All volunteers who enter HCPSS schools to interact with or support students must provide proof of vaccination status or a negative COVID-19 test. Parents and other visitors who enter schools for a brief visit are not required to show proof of vaccination or testing. All parents, visitors, and volunteers are required to be masked, regardless of their vaccination status.

 {{> news-item title="Updated Links: Student Climate Change Institute Applications" message="

Applications Due: Tuesday, September 28

HCPSS sophomores are invited to apply for an eight-week Student Climate Change Institute, cosponsored by the Howard County Conservancy and HCPSS. Selected participants will have opportunities to learn from and collaborate with climate change experts, environmental decision makers and other students during weekly sessions in the fall. In the spring, students will meet monthly to implement action projects.

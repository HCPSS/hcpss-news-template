 {{> news-item title="Instructional Supports for Students in Quarantine " message="

Students who must quarantine have several opportunities to continue learning and receive supports, which will help them easily transition back to instruction once they return to school.

" }} {{> news-item title="Vaccination expectations for students, staff and visitors" message="

Full vaccination or regular COVID-19 testing will be required of all HCPSS staff. Students and visitors do not need to provide proof of vaccination or testing. Find information on requirements for volunteers. All persons must wear masks while in school buildings.

" }} {{> news-item title="Contact Tracing and Quarantine Procedures" message="

Learn about the procedures that take place when a student or staff member is identified as a close contact or identified with COVID-like illness or infection.

" }} {{> news-item title="Inclement Weather Closings and Notifications" message="

Find out where announcements of closings and delays are shared and how parents can receive notifications. HCPSS does not transition to virtual instruction during weather emergencies.

" }} {{> news-item title="Providing Consent for In-School COVID Testing" message="

HCPSS offers free, voluntary COVID tests for students and school-based staff who develop COVID-like symptoms or are identified as close contacts while they are in a school building. Parents/guardians who have not already done so can provide consent in HCPSS Connect Synergy to enable your child to participate in this testing program.

" }} {{> news-item title="Support for technology-related issues" message="

Find directions for connecting to WiFi, retrieving a user name or password, and other self-help resources.

" }} {{> news-item title="Technology Support Center update" message="

The Technology Support Center at the Old Cedar Lane building in Columbia has reopened. Students may visit that site or the Tech Support Center at Old Bushy Park location. Details on Technology Support can be found online.

" }} {{> news-item title="COVID-19 Self-Screenings" message="

All parents/guardians are to perform a COVID-19 health check every day before your student leaves home. Students are not to come to school if they are experiencing any symptom(s) associated with COVID-19 or other communicable disease.

Reminders

 {{> news-item title="Cafeteria Protocols Update" message="

To reduce the risk of the spread of COVID-19, all schools will have outdoor eating spaces available for student use as weather and staffing allow. Additional tables, tents, and plexiglass shields are being procured to maintain a healthy environment in cafeterias.

" }} {{> news-item title="Student/Family Chromebook Responsibilities" message="

Beginning Monday, September 13, HCPSS will charge families for lost, stolen or damaged devices. Details on how families can report a device incident and how charges will be handled can be found online.

" }} {{> news-item title="Student Climate Change Institute" message="

Students entering 10th grade are invited to apply by Tuesday, September 28 for an eight-week Student Climate Change Institute, cosponsored by the Howard County Conservancy and HCPSS. Selected participants will have opportunities to learn from and collaborate with climate change experts, environmental decision makers and other students during weekly sessions in the fall. In the spring, students will meet monthly to implement action projects.

 {{> news-item title="Board of Education Adopts Requested Operating, Capital Budgets" message="

The Board adopted its FY2022 Operating and Capital Budget Requests and FY 2023-27 Capital Improvement Program. The requests will be submitted to the County Executive in March. The Board will adopt its final budgets on May 27. The Operating Budget request includes funding to meet obligations including staff compensation, student transportation, and enrollment growth; as well as to sustain the recent investments in technology and software, address critical shortages in special education, and to support student mental health services and dyslexia and reading instruction. The Capital Budget request includes funding for the Talbott Springs ES Replacement, New High School #13, Hammond HS Renovation and Addition, and other capital projects

At 93.4%, the graduation rate for the HCPSS Class of 2020 exceeded the Maryland average of 86.8%, and is up from 92.8% for 2019 and from 92.0% in 2018. While graduation rate gaps persist among student demographic groups, several groups showed notable improvement compared to the prior year.

 {{> news-item title="Updated Attendance Procedures" message="

Effective March 1, a student who attends class in person or synchronously Monday, Tuesday, Thursday and Friday will be marked present. A student will be marked absent if they do not attend instruction in person or virtually. Find additional information, including attendance procedures on Wednesdays.

" }} {{> news-item title="Update to Student Meal Service" message="

Effective March 1, meals are available for pickup from 7:45-8:45 a.m. at elementary school locations and 9-10 a.m. at middle and high school locations on specified days. There will be no pickups on Tuesdays and Fridays. Students and parents/guardians have the option to receive meals at their child’s school or at any designated meal distribution site. Additional details about changes to meal service are available online.

" }} {{> news-item title="Non-Instructional Video Streaming Sites Not Accessible" message="

Video streaming services—such as Netflix, TikTok, Hulu, Showtime, Snapchat, Tumblr, Xbox live, Fortnite and HBO—are no longer accessible on the HCPSS network or on HCPSS-issued devices. These services are not approved instructional technology sites and use of these sites has created network performance issues.

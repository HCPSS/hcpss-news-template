" }} {{> news-item title="HCPSS’ Multilingual Parent Leadership Program Applications Open" message="

HCPSS’ six-week Multilingual Parent Leadership Program is designed to help multilingual parents navigate the school system and develop the confidence to serve as advocates for their children. The program also provides training for parents to assume leadership roles in schools and the community. Interested participants must complete an application by Sept. 28.

" }} {{> news-item title="Asynchronous Instruction Update" message="

The Board of Education voted not to accept the HCPSS 2023-2024 Inclement Weather Asynchronous Instruction Plan. As such, there will be no option for asynchronous instruction days during inclement weather events this school year.
